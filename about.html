Astroworld is the third studio album by rapper Travis Scott. It was released on August 3, 2018. The album includes 17 songs, "SICKOMODE" being the most "popular" one. HE began his US tour on November 8th in Baltimore, Maryland, ending it on December 19 in Los Angeles, California. According to Rolling Stones,' Astroworld: Wish You Were Here' tour is the greatest show on Earth. Travis does the most to make every single one of his shows extremely hype and unforgettable. During the show, he rides a roller coaster and has an actual ferris wheel on stage! Kylie Jenner and their daughter Stormi Webster come to all the shows and occasionally make appearances on stage! During the show that took place in NYC, Kylie rode the roller coaster alongside Travis!
